Guide & Key
Views
📍
Map — Activations
Shows every bookable space on the floor plan. Pins show the brand currently booked. Use the month tabs to step through the year.
📊
Heat Map
Sales performance overlay. Cards show MTD sales, vs last month, vs last year, margin, and profit. Switch between Spaces and Brands in the sidebar.
📅
Calendar
Spreadsheet view of the master data — all brands, promos, and notes in row format.
📋
Tech View
Switches map pins to show space name, player ID, and screen spec. Only shows spaces with a spec defined.
Map Pins
Blue — Activation
Brand-booked activation fixture. Standard endcap, HPP, or counter space.
Green — Store Owned
DFA-managed fixture. Often windows, entrances, or permanent brand displays.
Dark Blue — Media Rental
Digital screen booked for ad content. LEDs, totems, gate displays, facade panels.
Red outline — Changed
Brand or promo has changed vs the prior month. Red dot also appears in the sidebar.

Sidebar Controls

Makes that layer's pins visible on the map.

Hides that layer's cards from the map without removing them from the sidebar.
